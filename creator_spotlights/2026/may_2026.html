May 2026 Creator Spotlight - Cotton Honey

This month, our Artist of the Month is Cotton Honey!

Watch the previous YouTube short here. May is coming soon!


Here's our interview with Cotton Honey:

- Can you introduce yourself and share a bit about your background?

My name is AkaineSunset i’m an artist 2D/3D from Mexico, i have 24 for years old almost 25, i am a system (DID), and i spend most of the time Creating 3D models and renders of any kind!, my life as an artist wasn’t easy like most of Artist, to be honest I wasn't going to be an artist, i was more into Mechanic, but i unfortunately didn’t fit the category so as the time pass i start by making 3D models for VRchat, soon i learn how to draw, then i discover i like the artist world too, i learn everything by myself until 2022 that i started university studying Animation and Art on Videogames, today I got my degree on 20 of april from the present year. I also have a Diploma in Advanced Graphic Design.

- What inspired you to become an artist?

In that time it inspired me to make my favorite characters. I was a fan of mlp amnesia and creepypastas, i found a classmate that drew and she was the one who made me get more into drawing. At first I started by making stickmans with hair and slowly started to make my own characters.

- How did you first discover Frutiger Aero as an aesthetic, and what drew you to it?

I born in that era, so for me its already nostalgic, i used windows XP and windows 7 yet i didn’t know it will become an aesthetic years later or even get a name, but as soon i knew i stay because it brings me peace and happiness remembering the games i used to play on the computers of my school trying to not get discovered by the teacher hahaha, i used to play Mi primera enciclopedia, Purple place, Mines, The Human body, Etc.

- How would you describe your art style?

I would Say 3D Glossy, i perfectionate the glossy material in maya and i practically use it in everything.

- Can you walk us through your creative process from idea to finished piece?

This one is Hard, usually i just play games while i listen random youtube music, as i hear different music styles sometimes one pic my attention and then my mind start to work, first by getting an Idea can be an animation, a character, a wallpaper, a model anything, then i draw the idea on paintool sai 2, if its an animation i draw the storyboard on my notebook, if its a model i search references, if its a wallpaper i just open maya, sometimes when is character creation i use pinterest to get inspiration for the clothes, posible abilities, accessories or weapons.

- What tools or mediums do you prefer to use, and why?

For 3D work Autodesk Maya, Unity, Blender, substance and Nuke. For 2D PaintoolSai, AfterEffects, ClipStudio, Cavalry and sometimes photoshop.

- How do you overcome creative blocks?

Play with friends, i stop overthinking in ideas its not easy but get yourself distracted from everything helps a lot, the less you force yourself to get ideas the less will stay the art block, sometimes i listen calm music or go outside.

- What inspires your work? Are there specific themes, emotions, or stories you aim to convey?

Well right now its Frutiger Aero, Dreamcore, Weridcore and backrooms, for emotions usually can be Love, happiness, calm but for some reason i make better works when im sad mostly because i think is the best state to concentrate no because is good being sad of course not! But i use it not to improve my own work but because i want to make myself happy, yes sounds weird but think of this…you are sad, something happen in your life and you are not in the best moment, if you do what you feel and you do it freely, you will see a change even if it looks bad or better you see a change and then you think on the positive part “hey…this work looks even better than my others, i improve!” or “this one looks bad but…I see my previous work i..really make a difference in my work, im improving”.

- Are there any artists, aesthetics, or movements that have influenced your style?

Only two artist and both are latinoamericans like me, Fernanda Diaz the creator of witch bunny and Wizzard Cat, Lili_cuan on twitter Their Art style is just Amazing and inspire me alot in my style today.

- What role does the Frutiger Aero aesthetic play in your art?

I would say Color theory and Rendering.

- What has been the most challenging piece you’ve created, and why?

My Aero City, Making the buildings, materials and then making the place, fixing the bones, the ground, the render and making the water was…a pain haha.

- Can you share a piece you’re especially proud of and why it’s significant to you?

I think one of my best works is a liminal espace i did of The pool rooms, it was the work of months to make the renders and textures.

- How has being part of the Frutiger Aero community influenced your artistic journey?

Very much, to be honest i was struggling with Floop, people stop looking at my art and it was hard for me to keep going but as soon i enter this community i got a lot of support, people who actually like my works, comments, i got a collaboration, i got offers to do this kind of interviews and it makes me really happy, i will continue my path in this community and mostly because i really enjoy doing Frutiger Aero content.

- What do you hope people take away from your art?

Detail, i really make a big effort in details on my art its a part i enjoy too much and most importantly...a visual break, i know frutiger aero has tons of colors, designs but i also want to make something clean, something calming no too much stickers, not too much icons or colors too bright that can hurt the eyes.

- Do you have any upcoming projects or goals you’re excited about?

Yes, im actually Working on a Animation of Frutiger Evil, im also working on wallpapers for nintendo DS and phone keyboards, More models of windows, Internet explorer and much more for people to use or maybe just maybe made some files to get printed with a 3D printer. I do have another thing working on but what is a Surprise.

- Where can people find more of your work or support you?

Tiktok mostly (akainesunset) and recently open a BlueSky account (@cottonhoney.bsky.social)

- If you could collaborate with any artist, living or dead, who would it be and why?

I think would be Michael Jakson, i mean he was a wonderful person, inspiring and very very supportive, for me would be a pleasure if it was possible.

- What’s a fun fact about you that most people wouldn’t know?

Uhhhh i make sounds when im focused deeply on something like…saying out of nowhere “blahblahblah” “lelelelele” or just making the sound of “pop pop pop” hahaha

- Do you have a favorite Frutiger Aero memory or moment?

Yesss the bubbles on the screen when the pc goes in Energy safe mode!, you can still do it in windows 11 but its not the same.

- If you could describe your artistic journey in three words, what would they be?

Freedom, nostalgic and Joyful.

- What advice would you give to aspiring artists who are inspired by Frutiger Aero?

Keep going, don’t worry about numbers, if its makes you happy do it, would be people who like it or people who don’t, would be people who want you get down but there will be more that support you, the artist path is not easy and would never be…but don’t do things for others, do it because you love to do it, don’t think in what others like, always do it for your happiness, your love for art and you will be in right path.

- Is there anything else you’d like to share with the Frutiger Aero community?

Just to thank them for all the support in this short time, i really really appreciate it so much, i have no words to thank enough but i will do my best to keep bringing breaks to everyone and giving more to this community.
