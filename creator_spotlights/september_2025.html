September 2025 Creator Spotlight - ElijahQ2010

This month, our Artist of the Month is ElijahQ2010!

Watch the YouTube short here.


Here's our interview with ElijahQ2010:

- Can you introduce yourself and share a bit about your background?

My name is Elijah, I’m a 15 years old male, and a sophomore in high school.

- What inspired you to become an artist?

I’ve been inspired to become an artist/graphic designer ever since I’ve discovered the Frutiger Aero aesthetic for the first time.

- How did you first discover Frutiger Aero as an aesthetic, and what drew you to it?

I’ve first discovered Frutiger Aero from a compilation on YouTube in March 2024, and what drew me to it is that it represents a hopeful vision of the future that people experienced during their childhood or early adulthood in the 2000s, and early 2010s.

- How would you describe your art style?

I would describe my art style as futuristic freestyle.

- Can you walk us through your creative process from idea to finished piece?

I’m a go with the flow type person when it comes to graphic designing.

- What tools or mediums do you prefer to use, and why?

I use stock images, and Canva to create my artwork.

- How do you overcome creative blocks?

Despite having creative blocks, I try to keep going, even if I’m not confident in hopes the final product is good.

- What inspires your work? Are there specific themes, emotions, or stories you aim to convey?

My designs convey feelings of nostalgia, hope, and comfort.

- Are there any artists, aesthetics, or movements that have influenced your style?

My inspirations are old technology, and user interface aesthetic.

- What role does the Frutiger Aero aesthetic play in your art?

The Frutiger Aero aesthetic is present in all of my designs from vibrant colors, realistic, and glossy textures.

- What has been the most challenging piece you’ve created, and why?

If I’m being honest, making my artwork is challenging part, it’s hard to be unique sometimes.

- Can you share a piece you’re especially proud of and why it’s significant to you?

This art piece is the one I’m most proud of to share, and it’s significant to me because it I’m improving overtime.

- How has being part of the Frutiger Aero community influenced your artistic journey?

Being in the Frutiger Aero community has taught me different tools, and how to properly edit my photos and gave me motivation to keep going.

- What do you hope people take away from your art?

I have hard time answering that question since I’m still new to art and graphic design. I would hope though that people will appreciate my designs, and come up with their own interpretations of it.

- Do you have any upcoming projects or goals you’re excited about?

One of my goals is to become a professional graphic designer.

- Where can people find more of your work or support you?

People can find my work on instagram @elijahq2010 and Reddit u/ElijahQ2010.

- If you could collaborate with any artist, living or dead, who would it be and why?

Not as of right now but I’m open to idea of collaborating.

- What’s a fun fact about you that most people wouldn’t know?

One fun fact about me is that I like aesthetics from cartoons, movies, and technology from the 2010s.

- Do you have a favorite Frutiger Aero memory or moment?

My favorite Frutiger Aero memory was seeing the old Windows 7, and Vista operating systems from the doctors office and home computer when I was younger.

- If you could describe your artistic journey in three words, what would they be?

Three words that I would describe my artistic journey is challenging, exciting, and emotional.

- What advice would you give to aspiring artists who are inspired by Frutiger Aero?

Being open to criticism.

- Is there anything else you’d like to share with the Frutiger Aero community?

What I would like to share is a thank you to everyone in the community for positive feedback, and support.
