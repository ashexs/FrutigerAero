Website Launched!

Welcome to the new community website!

To celebrate our awesome community, we decided to create this website, to host our various community content and news announcements, among other things. You will be able to find outgoing links to the various community places that we maintain, such as the Discord server, the Subreddit, and much more.

You can also learn more about the aesthetic, find cool videos to watch, and read various interviews of Frutiger Aero Artists in the Creator Spotlights section!

How it's been done

The website was designed in Figma by Solinus. It is a clear tribute to the Frutiger Aero aesthetic, with colors resembling Windows Vista notably with the background, transparency, and glossiness of the interface. The design is reminiscent of the AERO interfaces of the early 2000s, but it also incorporates modern elements such as mobile responsiveness and accessibility features.

Once we decided on the design (and months of procrastination), I (Daniele63, who is also writing this article) started the coding part, which brings the vision to life. Now I had some coding experience from working on the Frutiger Aero Archive and my own personal website, but I'm still learning. It has been coded in vanilla HTML/CSS and JavaScript.

The rest of the staff also helped with the writing of other pages, notably the Creator Spotlights and Community Values. We can also thank m.e.z.z.o for our mascot "Aqua the Android".

Conclusion

We hope you will enjoy this website as much as we did making it! If you find any issues or bugs, please report them to us by sending us an e-mail. Feel free to suggest things you want to see added as well!
