About Us

The Consumer Aesthetics Network is a group of people passionate about old aesthetics such as Frutiger Aero, Frutiger Metro, Y2K, and others.
You can find us on all the major social media, as well as our active and growing community spaces.

FAQ

  • Q: What is Frutiger Aero?

  • A: You can learn more about it in the Resources section, or look at the Aesthetics Wiki, who also describes its sub-aesthetics.

  • Q: How to be featured in the Creator Spotlights?

  • A: You must join our Discord server and post your art there, or apply here. The winners are decided by the admins each month.

  • Q: Where to buy Frutiger Aero-style products

  • A: We recommend using eBay or buying secondhand. For Aqua mouses, Conaqua is a good option.

  • Q: Where to find Frutiger Aero Wallpapers and Music

  • A: You can find music on our YouTube and Spotify playlists. For wallpapers, you can visit the Frutiger Aero Archive.
